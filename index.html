I'm Mohammad Raihan Uddin, a Master’s student in Computer Engineering at The University of Alabama in Huntsville and an enthusiastic researcher at the Networking, Intelligence, and Security Lab under the supervision of Dr. Dinh C. Nguyen. I completed B.Sc in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering from Daffodil International University in Dhaka under advisor Dr. Md. Taslim Arefin, and since then, I've been deeply involved in the complexities of ML, AI, privacy, and security, Wireless Communication. Since 2015, I have been an active IEEE member, contributing to both the Bangladesh Chapter and the IEEE Region 3, Huntsville Section. My role in IEEE has allowed me to bridge communities and enhance student engagement through numerous conferences and events across Bangladesh, India, and Nepal. I am passionate about creating platforms for intellectual exchange, demonstrated by my role in organizing TEDxUA Huntsville 2024, and my leadership as the President of the UAH Bangladeshi Student Association and Secretary of the UAH Graduate Student Association In my commitment to promoting leadership and dialogue among youth, I proudly serve as the President of the House of Youth Dialogue (HYD). This organization focuses on empowering young leaders through Model United Nations conferences and various leadership forums, fostering skills in diplomacy and public speaking. My technical skills are broad and include proficiency in Python, MATLAB, and C, along with expertise in PyTorch, Numpy, Pandas, TensorFlow, and Scikit-Learn. I am also certified in AWS, Docker, and Kubernetes, underpinning my strong foundation in managing and deploying modern IT solutions. Beyond the lab and lectures, I engage in a variety of community and extracurricular activities. I am a co-founder of “Toru Chaya”, an organization dedicated to aiding rural children, and I pursue my passions for theatre, cycling, and photography in my free time. Through my experiences, I strive to contribute effectively to the advancement of technology while ensuring it serves the betterment of society globally. Join me on my journey as I continue to explore the intersections of technology, education, and community service.

Research Interest

ML, AI, IoT, Privacy & Security, and Wireless Communication.
