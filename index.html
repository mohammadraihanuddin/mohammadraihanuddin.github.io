I'm Mohammad Raihan Uddin, a Master’s student in Computer Engineering at The University of Alabama in Huntsville and an enthusiastic researcher at the Networking, Intelligence, and Security Lab under the supervision of Dr. Dinh C. Nguyen. I completed B.Sc in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering from Daffodil International University in Dhaka under advisor Dr. Md. Taslim Arefin, and since then, I've been deeply involved in the complexities of ML, AI, privacy, and security, Wireless Communication. Since 2015, I have been an active IEEE member, contributing to the Bangladesh Chapter and the IEEE Region 3, Huntsville Section. My role in IEEE has allowed me to bridge communities and enhance student engagement through numerous conferences and events across Bangladesh, India, and Nepal. I am passionate about creating platforms for intellectual exchange, demonstrated by my role in organizing TEDxUA Huntsville 2025, and my leadership as the President of the UAH Bangladeshi Student Association and Secretary of the UAH Graduate Student Association. In my commitment to promoting leadership and dialogue among youth, I proudly serve as the President of the House of Youth Dialogue (HYD). This organization focuses on empowering young leaders through Model United Nations conferences and various leadership forums, fostering skills in diplomacy and public speaking.

My technical skills include proficiency in Python, MATLAB, and C, along with expertise in PyTorch, Numpy, Pandas, TensorFlow, and Scikit-Learn. I am also certified in AWS, Docker, and Kubernetes, underpinned by a strong foundation in managing and deploying modern IT solutions.
