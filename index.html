As an M.Sc. Student in Computer Engineering at The University of Alabama in Huntsville. Since 2015, I have been an active member of IEEE, initially with the Bangladesh Chapter and now with IEEE Region 3, Huntsville Section, while pursuing my M.Sc. My commitment to IEEE is evident through my participation in conferences and chapter activities in both regions. I have collaborated with sponsors and industrial partners to boost community engagement and organized over 20 student events at Model United Nations conferences across Bangladesh, India, and Nepal. Beyond IEEE, I serve as the elected Secretary of the UAH Graduate Student Association and President of the UAH Bangladeshi Student Association. Additionally, I have obtained the license to organize TEDxUA Huntsville in 2024, showcasing my dedication to promoting intellectual and cultural exchange within the university and the broader community.
