I am a Ph.D. student at Clemson University (expected Dec 2028) and a member of the IS-WiN Laboratory, advised by Dr. Fatemeh Afghah. My research focuses on machine learning, federated learning, trustworthy AI, and intelligent wireless networks.

I previously worked in the Networking, Intelligence and Security (NIS) Lab at The University of Alabama in Huntsville, where I completed my MSE in Electrical Engineering (expected Dec 2025) under Dr. Dinh C. Nguyen.

Current Research Focus: I work on federated and quantum federated learning, LLM-assisted reasoning for ORAN/6G systems, and secure learning methods for wireless infrastructures.
